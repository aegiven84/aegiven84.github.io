Treehouse Art Studio Offerings

The Treehouse Art Studio is located inside the Center for Healing Arts on the 3rd Floor. The studio offers Open Studio sessions, Create and Sip events, and is available for contract for workshops, meetings, birthday parties, and other events. Most events are led by Darcie Taggart, ATR-BC, LPAT, LPCC the founder of the studio. Please contact Darcie at 502-208-4062, option 2 or via email at darcie.artscounseling@gmail.com for reservations, more information, or to schedule an appointment. For more information on upcoming events at the Treehouse Art Studio please visit us at : www.facebook.com/treehousestudiolouisville

Open Studio Art Sessions

Open Studio Art Sessions are a time for creation, exploration, and connection in the studio space. During an Open Studio session individuals are invited to create whatever is in their imaginations, heart, and soul from a variety of media available. These sessions are scheduled twice a month but are also available by appointment.

Kids Open Studio sessions are scheduled the 2nd Saturday of each month from 10am - 1pm and are open to kids ages 5 - 12 years. Participants are asked to bring a sack lunch to each session. These sessions are by appointment only and require a reservation as space is limited. A variety of topics will be explored though participants will be invited to create whatever they desire with a variety of materials. Cost per session is $20 with a half off discount offered to a second sibling or friend that is registering at the same time.

Open Studio sessions are scheduled the 1st Saturday of each month from 12 - 5pm and are open to all ages. Children under 12 are asked to participate with an adult for these events. The only exception to this is that the 1st Saturday in May, the studio will be open from 10am - 2pm instead of the regular hours to accomodate the KY Derby festival. Open studio cost is $10 per participant and you are invited to stay as long as you like until closing.

Create and Sip Events

Create and Sip Events are offered on Friday evenings from 6-9pm. These can be scheduled for a group or may at times be offered for couples and individuals to register. Create and sip events are offered periodically or by reservation. Please see the facebook page for current offerings.

Workshops, Meetings, Events, and Parties

The Treehouse Art Studio is available for rent for Workshops, Meetings, and Events on an hourly basis. The cost to rent the studio for a Workshop, Meeting, or Event is $25 per hour with an additional materials fee if art supplies are being used.

Birthday parties are offered in two hour slots with an activity and all art materials provided. The fee for a party is $100 for up to 8 participants with an additional $5 fee per participant over 8. Additional cost for specific materials may apply such as paint canvases. Parents or party planners are responsible for refreshments during the party. Please contact Darcie for additional details or to make a reservation.
