Arts-Based Life Coaching

The arts bring a whole new dimension to life coaching. Instead of just talking about your goals and planning a strategy to achieve them, coach Deb Ogburn will help you use the arts to rewire your brain for success. Typically, sessions take place in a studio setting where you will have a variety of arts media to augment your process. If you are not in the Louisville Metro area, Skype or FaceTime sessions can be arranged.

Arts-based life coaching is helpful for the following and much more:

  • Stress management and balance
  • Personal growth
  • Relationships and intimacy
  • Career planning
  • Entrepreneurial challenges
  • Life transitions
  • Self-care, health & aging
  • Creative blocks for artists, writers, musicians
  • Burnout

There is no need for artistic “talent” or experience in the arts to benefit.
