Expressive Arts Therapy

Expressive Arts Therapy employs art-making as a vehicle to express feelings that are difficult to put into words. The process of giving physical form to experiences and emotions helps to contain them and reduce their effect. Our highly trained practitioners are skilled in the therapeutic use of the arts to help you release the past and create a brighter, healthier future.

Expressive arts therapy can help clients of all ages. Often used as a treatment for trauma, expressive arts therapy is also helpful in reducing stress or anxiety caused by change and uncertainty in daily life, grieving the loss of a loved one, or facing a terminal illness. Sessions are held in an art studio setting where you will have a variety of materials to choose from to explore and convey your feelings or situation. In addition to visual arts, sound/music, dance/movement, poetry, journaling and drama may be used to augment your therapy. There is no need for artistic “talent” or experience in the arts to benefit.
