Darcie Taggart, ATR-BC, LPAT, LPCC

Individual, Family, and Group Art therapy and Counseling

Play Therapy

Founder of the Treehouse Art Studio

Open Studio for kids and adults

Workshops, Contract Groups, and Parties

Darcie is a Licensed Art Therapist and Clinical Counselor with a passion for working with children and families. She received a Bachelors degree in FIne Arts and Photography from the University of Tulsa in Tulsa, Oklahoma and a Master's degree in Expressive Therapy from the University of Louisville. She has been working in the field of Mental Health counseling since 2002 and for the past 7 years has specialized in working with young children and their families as well as with children diagnosed with developmental delays and autism.

Darcie provides art therapy and counseling for individuals, familes, and groups as well as play therapy and parent coaching. She enjoys assisting individuals along their creative journey and believes all people have the power to create art and gain energy and healing from the creative process. Therapy sessions typically last 45 minutes to an hour and participants will have a variety of mediums available for self expression and discovery. Parent coaching sessions typically occur in a family session though sometimes Darcie will meet with parents individually to discuss family needs and current difficulties.

Darcie was a volunteer at Enchanted Arts Studio for the past three years where she worked with adults and children to provide and Open Studio space for creativity and exploration of art media of all kinds. She is excited to be continuing that work with the Treehouse Art Studio which is located inside the Center for Healing Arts. The studio offers Open Studio for kids once a month, Open Studio for all once a month, monthly Sip and Create events, and is available for groups, workshops, and parties on a contracted basis. Darcie will also provide arts based groups and workshops in your setting on a contract basis.

Fees for services vary depending on the type of service provided but Darcie strives to maintain reasonable rates for all services to make therapy and art creation affordable for all. Therapy is based on a sliding scale fee depending on family income and rates for events at the Treehouse Art Studio are intentionally kept low to make services more affordable. Please contact Darcie via telephone at 502-208-4062, or by email at darcie.artscounseling@gmail.com for additional information on rates or to schedule an appointment.
