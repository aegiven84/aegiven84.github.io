Darcie Taggart

Darcie Taggart is a Licensed Art therapist and Licensed Professional Counselor with a passion for working with children and families. She has a bachelors degree in Fine Arts and Photography from the University of Tulsa in Oklahoma and a Master's degree in Expressive Therapy from the University of Louisville. For the past 12 years she has been working at Seven Counties and for the past 7 has been specializing in working with very young children and their families as well as children with developmental delays and autism. Darcie provides art therapy and play therapy interventions as well as parent coaching. She enjoys assisting individuals along their creative journey and believes all people have the power to create art and gain energy from the creative process. Darcie was a volunteer with Enchanted Arts Studio for it's three years in business where she worked with adults and children to provide an open art studio space for creativity and exploration in art media of all kinds. She hopes to continue some of that work in the new studio space at the Center for Healing Arts.
