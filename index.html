Welcome!

The Center for Healing Arts provides a holistic approach to health and healing. We are a collaborative and community-based health center that focuses on the well being of the whole person and/or family.

Our practitioners offer an array of services that incorporate mental health, physical health, emotional health and spiritual well-being. At Center for Healing Arts we offer a nurturing and compassionate environment cultivating creativity and genuine connection.

We invite you to experience restoration, healing and wholeness with us.
